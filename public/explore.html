Best Taste of Pho Saigon

Location

Vietnam might not mean that much to you, but it means a lot to the native owners of Best Taste of Pho Saigon located in Blaine, Minnesota. The restaurant serves delicious and authentic Vietnamese cuisine. Eating up their fresh pho is one of the most amazing foods to warm you on a cold Minnesota winter evening. Also, explore other Vietnamese food like egg rolls, Bahn mi, and different stir frys. There is no shame at Pho Saigon for digging into your noodles and slurping your soup a little too loud before letting out a gasp of satisfaction. So next time you’re craving some yummy Vietnamese food, head into Best Taste of Pho Saigon!

Freshly Leaf Boba

Location

Got a good score on a test and looking for a way to treat yourself? Look no further than Freshly Leaf Boba. This newly founded business has freshly-brewed teas, boba drinks, and smoothies that are perfect for your sweet tooth. The ingredients are high quality and the menu provides a wide variety of different drinks. Customers can either discover new flavors or if you're bored… then simply enjoy your favorites again and again. So next time you get a good score go out to Freshly Leaf and treat yourself!

Honour Coffee and Raw Juice

Location

“Woahhhh be careful don’t slip” warned the banana safety cone in the corner of Honour Coffee and Raw Juice. Honour Coffee values the importance of a good morning ritual and the best way to start the day is with a fresh cup of coffee. At Honour Coffee they support local Costa Rican coffee bean producers. The small business ensures that the beans are treated with care throughout the whole process to bring out the bright natures and best flavor of the bean to your coffee cup. Come to Honour Coffee to enjoy their hand crafted coffee and remember don’t slip when you are inside!

Mojo Coffee Gallery

Location

Do you need a place to do your homework? Perhaps unwind a bit and catch up with friends? Mojo Coffee Gallery is the place to be! The location is ideal for a bucket list cafe because it combines live music and vintage art in one location. Various art and pottery pieces are displayed and available for purchase along the walls. The cozy ambiance creates a welcoming atmosphere that is calming to all.

Cafe Meow

Location

Feeling just a tad bit lonely? In need of some relaxation? Well here's the purr-fect place to go for your next trip out. Cafe Meow is the first cat cafe in Minnesota, boasting a wide array of adorable cats alongside a menu of baked goods and coffee. When people say that time passes by quickly when you’re having fun, they aren’t lying. Even an hour spent there can feel only like a couple of minutes. So, book online and have a guaranteed paw-sitive time.

Weisman Art Museum

Location

Perhaps you were driving past the University of Minnesota campus and wondered what that oddly shaped, silver structure was. Well, I can say you’re not the only one. Weisman Art Museum stands out between all the buildings and college kids, but once you’re inside you can’t even tell you’re on a college campus. All of the art pieces are culturally varied and come from a variety of backgrounds. An exhibit that was shown was Foundling: 100 Days which showcased portraits of adopted children from different countries such as South Korea, Russia, and many others.
